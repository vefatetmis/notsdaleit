## Tam açıklama — İNGİLİZCE

notsdaleit combines note-taking, PDF markup and pen drawing in one app.
Ad-free, free and open source.

✏️ WRITE & DRAW ON THE SAME PAGE
• Rich text: bold, italic, underline/strike, bullet & check lists
• Font family and size selection
• Pen, highlighter and eraser with color palette and stroke widths
• A4 or square pages; white, yellow, green and black paper

📄 VIEW & ANNOTATE PDFs
• Import PDFs and draw on top of pages
• Export your notes as PDF and share them

🤝 LIVE SHARED NOTES
• Share a note with a 6-character code; the other person joins with it
• Strokes appear on the other device in real time; text syncs in seconds
• No account, no sign-up, no password — the code is enough

📅 CALENDAR & REMINDERS
• Add tasks to a day, pick a time, get notified right on time
• Free-form notes per day

🔁 ROUTINES
• Habit checklists that reset daily (or on the weekdays you choose)
• See your history on a monthly calendar

🔒 PRIVACY FIRST
• All data stays on your device; the app is fully offline
• No ads, no analytics, no tracking
• Data is transmitted only if you use live sharing, and only for that note

🌗 Light/dark theme · 🇹🇷 Turkish & 🇬🇧 English · Template (.ntdl) import/export

Open source: https://github.com/vefatetmis/notsdaleit
